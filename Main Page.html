Comic Morals Wiki

About Comic Morals Wiki:

For Comic-Fans of all ages, this site is here to explain and breakdown the morality and motivations of your comic book characters from any known genre along with stating the known crimes they have potentially committed and how they can be justified.

However, this does not include characters that feature in tie-in comics that are set in the same continuity of Movie/Tv/Video-game franchises, unless the tory/continuity they stem from is separate from the source material of said franchise.

To start with, heres a set of lists to chose from about your favorite comic-book characters and see what kind of moral compas they have, if they have one to begin with.
