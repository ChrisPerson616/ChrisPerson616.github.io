Green Goblin

(Info from both Marvel and Villains Wiki Fandom)

Norman Osborn, also well known as the Green Goblin, along with other known ailas, is one of the main major antagonists from Marvel Comics. He is also the main anotagonist of the main spiderman comic book series.

He is the founder and CEO of the multinational corporation Oscorp and father of Harry Osborn, Peter Parker's best friend, who became the first and most iconic incarnation of the Green Goblin after ingesting a serum, known as the Goblin Formula that increased his physical abilities yet in the process drove him to insanity.

Throughout the history of Marvel Comics, the Green Goblin has become the most personal arch-enemy of Spider-Man, due to playing a significant part of the misery and hardship in Spider-Man's life than any other villians in his long list of rouges gallery.

Despite being recognised as one of Spider-Man's arch-enemy, he has frequently funded campains to antagonize other heroes in the Marvel Universe, especially the Avengers. He even served as the main villainous protagonist of Marvel's Dark Reign storyline.

Motivations:

Norman Osborn's primary motivation is simply to acquire mass wealth and power, long before he ever became the Green Goblin. This is mostly stemmed from his childhood, as an extreme attempt to avoid the failures of his father, who was a strugling business inventor and took out his frustrations and anger out his familiy. After being exposed to the Goblin Formula, it not only enhanced his physical abilities to superhuman levels, but also caused him to develop a split personality, reprisenting his twisted side. All of these traits combined, creates a superpowered insane and sadistic mastermind who possess an extreme obsession of power and a drive to eleminiate anyone that he deems a threat to his plans, while expressing joy in tormenting Peter Parker/Spider-Man in anyway he can.

Notable Crimes and Questionable Deeds Committed:

  • Conspiracy

    Throughout the years, Osborn has planned muliptle campains for his own personal gain that caused a lot of harmful repercussions to everyone regardless if they were involed or not.

  • Terrorism

    Most of the actions he has done throughout the years as the Green Goblin can be labeled as terrorism, terrorising the citizens of NewYork all because of his cause of gaining power and sewing misery onto Spider-Man

  • Mass Murder

    Norman is reponsible of murdering untold innocent civilians and bystanders throughout his muliptle campains against Superheroes. In the story 'The Night Gwen Stacy Died', he is responsible of intentionally causing the death of Gwen Stacy, Peter Parker's first girlfriend, by dropping her from the George Washington Bridge, casusing Spider-Man to attempt to catch her with his webbing, however the wiplash casued by both the fall and Spider-Man's web catching her, ultimately broke her neck, killing her. This event would haunt Spider-Man throught the years.

  • Corruption

    Throughout the years, Norman has exploited various organisations into working for his own personal gain, spefically his own company OsCorp. In the story arc, 'Dark Riegn' He became the Director of S.H.I.E.L.D., shortly after the Secret Invasion Story arc, where he remolded it into H.A.M.M.E.R. and used his newlyfound posistion and public image as "America's Greatest Hero" to presue a set of campains against the Superhero community by hunting them down while forming his own team of Avengers that consits of Supervillains under the guise of heroes, publicly known as the Dark Avengers. He would eventually lose this posistion when he attempted to use it to invade Asgaurd in the 'Siege' story arc.

  • Weapons and Drug Manufacturing

    The Green Goblin is known for for his Goblin Themed weapons and gadgets, such his signiture Pumpkin bombs and his Goblin Glider that he manufactured himself and use them to cause mass destruction and chaos whereever he goes. He is also responsible for the creation of the very Goblin Formula that gave him his powers and drove him insane. Varios individuals had also use the goblin formula to become the Green Goblin, such as his own son Harry Osborn and even the HobGoblin. Due to its side effects, it can labeled as a drug and therefore it would be considered drug manufacturing.

  • Mass Destruction, Property Damage, Vandalism and Public Endangerment

    In his campains against Superheroes, Norman is responsible for causing untold amount of damage throughout NewYork, casusing a vast amount property damge in the city, while endangering many lives in the process.

  • Child Abuse and Negligence

    Norman has frequently emotionly abused his son Harry, throughout the majority of his childhood. Despite having to provide his son with everything he needed, through his wealth, he still negligeted him emotionly, depriving him of any attention or affection as he focused more on his work. This caused his son to sabotage his experment out of frustration and inevitably caused Norman's transformation into the Green Goblin.

  • Muliptle Acounts of Attempted Murder

    He has made muliptle attempts into killing superheroes, especially Spider-Man on many ocations.

    In the Siege Story Line, Norman as the Iron Patriot, along with H.A.M.M.E.R. and the Dark Avengers tried to invade Asgaurd, under the manipulation by Loki, casusing the entire land to crash land on Oklahoma in ruins, temporarily leaving the Asgaurdians without a home.
